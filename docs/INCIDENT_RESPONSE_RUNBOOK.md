# Incident Response Runbook

## Severity

- SEV-1: Active breach, critical data exposure, production compromise.
- SEV-2: High-impact security incident with containment in progress.
- SEV-3: Limited impact issue or policy violation.

## Procedure

1. Detect and triage.
2. Contain affected systems.
3. Preserve evidence/logs.
4. Assess impact scope and data categories.
5. Notify legal/privacy lead.
6. Decide regulator/user notification obligations.
7. Remediate and recover.
8. Publish postmortem and prevention actions.

## Notification

- Follow applicable legal deadlines for supervisory authority and user notifications.
- Maintain timeline of decisions and communications.

## Required Artifacts

- Incident timeline
- Impact assessment
- Containment/remediation actions
- Notification records
- Post-incident control improvements
